About CATS

One of the best things about cats is that they are generally very independent creatures. Some more than others.

Unlike dogs, who require quite a high level of attention each day, cats are quite happy to have time to themselves. In fact, cats will sleep for about 15 hours a day so you don't have to worry about them being too bored when you're at work.

But when you do decide to activate them, cats can be a very versatile pet! Cats can learn tricks and go on walks. They can even do agility (jumping over obsticales) or do circus acts!
